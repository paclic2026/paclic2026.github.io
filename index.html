About PACLIC

The Pacific Asia Conference on Language, Information and Computation (PACLIC) is a premier annual international conference that has brought together researchers in linguistics, computational linguistics, and natural language processing for four decades. Since 1987, PACLIC has served as a vital forum for advancing language science across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.

PACLIC 40 will be hosted in Taipei, Taiwan ‚Äî a vibrant intellectual and cultural hub where cutting-edge technology meets rich linguistic diversity. This milestone 40th edition invites scholars worldwide to explore the intersections of language, computation, and cultural diversity in the age of artificial intelligence.

Submit Your Research

Call for Papers

PACLIC 40 invites submissions of original research papers in linguistics, computational linguistics, natural language processing, and related disciplines. We welcome both long and short paper submissions.

Topics of Interest

PACLIC 40 invites papers addressing (but not limited to) the following areas:

  • Phonology, morphology, and syntax
  • Semantics and pragmatics
  • Discourse analysis and information structure
  • Computational linguistics and NLP
  • Machine translation and language technology
  • Large language models and AI
  • Language acquisition and psycholinguistics
  • Sociolinguistics and language variation
  • Corpus linguistics and lexicography
  • Information retrieval and text mining
  • Knowledge representation and reasoning
  • Sign language linguistics
  • Language documentation and endangered languages
  • Asian languages and linguistic typology

Submission Guidelines

Paper Types
  • Long Papers: 8‚Äì10 pages (+ references)
  • Short Papers: 4‚Äì6 pages (+ references)
Format

ACL anthology style. Templates available in Word and LaTeX format (links TBA).

Anonymization

All submissions must be double-blind anonymous. Remove author names, affiliations, and acknowledgements.

Language

Submissions must be in English.

Originality

Submitted papers must describe original, unpublished work not currently under review elsewhere.

Submission System

Submissions will be handled via CMT (link to be announced). Please check back before the submission deadline.

Submission Portal (Coming Soon)

Publication Information

Accepted papers will be published in the Proceedings of PACLIC 40 through the Institute for the Study of Language and Information (ISLI) at Kyung Hee University. Proceedings will be archived and made available online. Selected papers may be invited for extended publication in a special journal issue.
