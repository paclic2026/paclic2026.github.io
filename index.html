About PACLIC

The Pacific Asia Conference on Language, Information and Computation (PACLIC) is a premier annual international conference that has brought together researchers in linguistics, computational linguistics, and natural language processing for four decades. Since 1987, PACLIC has served as a vital forum for advancing language science across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.

PACLIC 40 will be hosted in Taipei, Taiwan, a vibrant intellectual and cultural hub where cutting-edge technology meets rich linguistic diversity. This milestone 40th edition invites scholars worldwide to explore the intersections of language, computation, and cultural diversity in the age of artificial intelligence.

Submit Your Research

Call for Papers

PACLIC 40 invites submissions of original research papers in linguistics, computational linguistics, natural language processing, and related disciplines.

Submission at a Glance

Paper Length

Up to 8 pages of content, excluding references and appendices.

Format

ACL 2026 two-column format using official ACL style files.

Review

Double-blind. Authors must not indicate names or affiliations.

Language

All submissions must be written in English.

Open Access ACL Anthology Scopus Indexed h5-index: 13
