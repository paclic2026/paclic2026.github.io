--- SECTION: ABOUT PACLIC | source: https://paclic2026.github.io ---
PACLIC (Pacific Asia Conference on Language, Information and Computation) is one of the longest-running academic conferences in the Asia-Pacific region dedicated to language science and language technology. It has been held annually since 1987, making PACLIC 40 in 2026 nearly four decades of continuous scholarship.

REPUTATION & STANDING:
- Scopus-indexed since PACLIC 19 (2005) — meets international peer-review and citation standards
- Listed in the ACL Anthology — the authoritative open archive for computational linguistics research
- Google Scholar h5-index: 13; h5-median: 18–19 (reflects citation impact of recent papers)
- Brings together researchers from linguistics, computational linguistics, and NLP across Asia, the Pacific, and beyond
- Papers undergo rigorous double-blind peer review
- Proceedings published in open-access digital format, freely available worldwide
- Past editions hosted by universities across Asia-Pacific (Japan, Korea, China, Philippines, Vietnam, Hong Kong, Singapore, etc.)

WHY ATTEND / SUBMIT:
- Ideal venue for work at the intersection of formal linguistics and computational/NLP approaches
- Strong community of Asia-Pacific researchers; excellent for networking in the region
- Open-access proceedings ensure your work is freely citable worldwide
- Both oral and poster presentations are considered equal in quality — format is based on the nature of the work
- PACLIC 40 is hosted by National Taiwan University (NTU), ranked among Asia's top research universities

GETTING TO TAIPEI FROM TAOYUAN AIRPORT (TPE):
Option 1 - Airport MRT (recommended): ~35–39 min, NT$160 (~USD 5), runs 6AM–11PM every 15 min. Take Express MRT to Taipei Main Station → transfer to Green Line → Gongguan Station (Exit 3), 3–5 min walk to NTU
Option 2 - Airport Bus 1819: ~70–90 min, NT$160, to Taipei Main Station then MRT/taxi to NTU
Option 3 - Taxi/Uber: ~40–50 min, NT$1,100–1,500 (~USD 35–48), available 24/7. Pickup at Columns 15/17/20/23 (T1) or 24/26/29 (T2)
From Songshan Airport (TSA): ~25–30 min, NT$30. Brown Line → Nanjing Fuxing → Green Line → Gongguan Station

GETTING AROUND TAIPEI:
- Taipei MRT: clean, safe, fully English-signposted. Nearest station: Gongguan Station (Green Line, Exit 3), 5-min walk to NTU. NT$20–65/trip, runs 6AM–midnight
- EasyCard (悠遊卡): rechargeable card for MRT, buses, YouBike, convenience stores. Get at any MRT station for NT$100
- YouBike: public bike-sharing, stations around NTU. First 30 min free, NT$10/30 min after. Requires YouBike 2.0 app enrollment
- Taxi/Uber/Line Taxi: metered taxis available on street. Starts NT$85, NT$20 surcharge 11PM–6AM. Have destination in Chinese characters ready

ACCOMMODATION (recommended areas):
- Da'an District: walking distance to NTU, quiet with good dining and MRT access
- Gongguan Area: directly adjacent to NTU, lively student district with affordable options
- Zhongzheng District: Taipei city centre, well-connected by MRT
- Zhongshan District: north of city centre, wide range of hotels at various price points
Hotel options will be listed on the website once finalised.

FOOD & DRINK:
Near NTU: Gongguan Night Market (5 min walk), Shida Night Market (10 min walk), Roosevelt Road restaurants, NTU campus cafeteria
Must-try Taiwanese foods: beef noodle soup, scallion pancake, oyster omelette, bubble tea (invented in Taiwan), Xiao Long Bao, stinky tofu
Vegan/vegetarian: look for 素 (Sù) character or Buddhist swastika (卍) on signs

TAIPEI HIGHLIGHTS:

- Daan Forest Park: peaceful urban forest minutes from NTU
- Ximending: vibrant pedestrian shopping and entertainment district
- Elephant Mountain (象山): 30-min hike with panoramic city views
